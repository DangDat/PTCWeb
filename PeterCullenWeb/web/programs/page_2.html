Women In Water Leadership Progam



The Program focuses on raising awareness of key issues amongst our mid-career rising stars and building leadership and communication skills specifically geared towards bringing about positive change in water and catchment management in Australia. The Trust selects participants who are highly skilled and motivated and can continue the work of Peter Cullen. The Program is a journey of personal discovery. It challenges participants to stretch themselves beyond their current boundaries and realise their potential to make a real difference in their chosen water related careers.
