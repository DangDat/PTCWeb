BRIDGING Newletters

Our quarterly newsletter, ‘BRIDGING’, is packed with interesting industry news and photos. BRIDGING informs readers of new projects, gives fascinating glimpses into the work of Fellows and Friends, reports on the leadership program and projects around the country.


The readership exceeds 50,000, including senior leaders and it is distributed across all States and Territories and sectors within the water and environment industry.


New issues of BRIDGING will be posted in our news items and on this page for back issue referencing.
